About Me

Specializing in lifting analysis for offshore installations, I analyze 3D models of offshore structures to accurately distribute physical properties and create representative models in OrcaFlex. This enables comprehensive static and dynamic analyses, culminating in the development of Hs tables that inform offshore vessels of the environmental conditions suitable for safe installation.

In addition to engineering analysis, I contribute to workflow optimization by developing automation scripts in Python, enhancing efficiency and streamlining processes within the installation analysis framework.
